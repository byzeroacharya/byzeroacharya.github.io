Inspired by the Olympic season, participants are asked to design a prototype torch that can be virtually lit representing mottos of their college and also choreograph and document a routine for the parade which will be held on Day '0'.

GUIDELINES

  • The torch should be light able utilising fire or artificial lights.
  • The torch should be passable and continuously lit throughout the duration of the parade.
  • The entire delegation must be a part of the parade.
  • Colleges are allowed to use any form of band and non-conventional instruments to create music.
  • A routine must be choreographed to showcase the alma mater slogans and create buzz.
  • The parade must be documented on Day '0' and submitted the next day on the morning of registration.
  • Usage of props are allowed, if permission is asked beforehand.

JUDGEMENT CRITERIA

  • Technicality and overall execution of torch design and parade (30%+30%)
  • Originality, creativity and energy during the parade.
  • Effectiveness to adhere to the rules.
